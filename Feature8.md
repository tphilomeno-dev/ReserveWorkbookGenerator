Feature 8 – Financial Projection

Goal

Generate the yearly financial history for a reserve fund.

Inputs

Reserve Schedule
Reserve Settings
Projection Settings

Outputs

Beginning Pool
Annual Contributions
Interest Earned
Reserve Expenditures
Ending Pool

Business Rules

Beginning Pool =
Previous Year's Ending Pool

Annual Contributions =
Sum of Reserve Schedule Contributions

Interest =
Interest Calculator

Reserve Expenditures =
Components reaching end-of-life

Ending Pool =
Beginning
+ Contributions
+ Interest
- Expenditures
